# PowerBi_coffe_shop_sales
# Final Insights

Total Sales: ~699K with 214K total quantity sold — strong overall performance.

Top Category: Coffee is the highest revenue contributor (~0.27M), followed by Tea and Bakery.

Low Performers: Packaged coffee and loose items contribute very little — candidates for review.

Top Locations: Sales are almost evenly split across Hell’s Kitchen, Astoria, and Lower Manhattan, with Hell’s Kitchen slightly leading.

Top Product Types: Barista Espresso and Brewed/Hot Chocolate categories generate the largest share.

Monthly Trend: Sales show a steady upward trend from January to June, peak in June — indicates seasonality or growth.

High-Selling Items: Bakery items like Almond Croissant and Chocolate Biscotti show high transaction quantities across all stores.

# What Was Used

Data Cleaning & Preparation: SQL / Power Query (remove nulls, fix types, standardize categories)

Data Modeling: Relationships between product, location, and transaction tables

Measures/KPIs: DAX measures for Total Sales, Quantity, Transactions

Visualization Tool: Power BI

Charts Used:

Bar charts → category & monthly sales

Pie/Donut charts → location & product type share

KPI cards → totals

Slicers → Month, Quarter, Product Category

Analysis Focus: Category performance, store comparison, product mix, and monthly trends
